Gabion Mattresses

An excellent solution provided by innovative research and use of modern technology, the Gabion Mattresses we offer are wire fabric containers that form various structures to control water flow and preserve soil. Gabion Mattresses are uniformly partitioned and of variable size, which is interconnected with similar containers that are filled with stone at the site of use. For getting the desired results, pre-formed stiffeners should be used at each exposed face of the gabion.

Manufactured Out Of

  • Woven Wire Fabric
Used For Making

  • Sea walls
  • Channel linings
  • Revetments
  • Weirs
Military Gabions

We provide a range of special Military Gabions that are required for various purposes. The Military Gabions we offer are modernized version of the early gabions, which are manufactured using latest technology to impart a high level of strength and durability. Providing protection to soldiers in sleeping quarters and mess halls, the Military Gabions can be made to stand on end and staked firmly in a position by filling soil to form a rock-solid wall.
